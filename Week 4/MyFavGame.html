Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games.The game is known for its immersive and detailed open world, which includes a wide range of activities and missions to complete. Players can engage in high-speed car chases, participate in shootouts with the police, and complete various heists and missions to advance the story. GTA V also features a robust online multiplayer mode, allowing players to team up with friends and compete in various activities.
